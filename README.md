# 🥗 Protein Intake Calculator

A simple web application that allows users to track their daily protein intake based on the foods they consume and check whether they have reached their recommended daily protein goal.

This app calculates protein intake from common foods and estimates the recommended protein requirement based on a user's body weight.

---



**The Solution: CalorieFlow**
CalorieFlow is a high-speed web application that transforms personal physical metrics into an actionable daily energy budget. By combining the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation with a curated, high-utility food database, the app provides a real-time "fuel gauge" for the human body. It tells the user not just how much they have eaten, but how much they specifically need to reach their physiological baseline.

## 🚀 Features

* Enter your **height and weight**
* Input the **amount of foods eaten today**
* Automatically **calculate total protein intake**
* Get **recommended daily protein intake**

* INNOVATION AND IMPACT
  What sets CalorieFlow apart is the Gap Analysis. Instead of just displaying a number, the app provides a status message. If a user is 500 calories under their goal, the app identifies this as an "Energy Deficit," which is crucial for users focused on muscle retention or cognitive performance.
